Create Your findMe Account

From the App Menu select Create new Account. Fill all the fields with the required information that is to say a working email Address or Your Contact number, Set A password for your Account, Agree to the terms and Conditions of finddMe App. Select Sign up. An email will be sent to your email Account that requires a confirmation for that email address. After confirming your email Account an account will be set for You.

Turn Location On From Your Settings

Head over to your device`s Settings and select Location, turn the location On for your device. This allows finddMe to Know your device`s location and uses it to allow other people you share your location with to locate you on the Google Maps. Hence establishing a route for easy access.

Share Your Location

In order to allow other people to locate you on the Google Maps, You need to share your lacation to them. Now you do this by selecting share location in the App. From here you can share your location to a single individual or a group of people depending on what you desire at the moment.

More Information

With finddMe once you dont stop your live share, the people you shared your location with will still be able to see your live location untill you disable live sharing. Once live sharing is stoped everyone you shared your location with will be able to see your last live location but cant see your current location at the time.

Its a good practice therefore to allways disable live sharing once your done with program
